Hello!

I'm Alex Renda, from the Bay Area in California. Currently, I'm pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from Cornell University, with an expected graduation date of May 2018.

I'm interested in all types of activites related to programming, such as robotics, Android app development, web development, and practically anything else involving challenging design problems.


Projects I've worked on:

All 4 years of high school, I was a member of my school's FRC team. We were given 6 weeks to create a robot that could perform tasks like shoot basketballs, pick up and throw Frisbees, and shoot two-foot diameter balls. I worked largely on the controls and autonomous modes of these robots, working with manual control, control loops, and machine vision.

I've also developed and released several Android apps. I've made a League of Legends information aggregator, a weight and balance calculator for small planes, and a non-linear tower defense game.
